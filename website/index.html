What is this?

This is Bananas' new wifi portal! All you need to do to connect to our free wifi is donate a portion of your computing power to the charity of your choice.

How does it work?

Using distributed computing, you join a grid of over one million users to help solve computational algorithms. Each user solves a small piece of a larger puzzle, be it searching for alien life, curing cancer, or decoding the human genome!

How do I get started?

Simple! Just select your charity of choice below and click connect. Make sure not to close this tab since it will be used to help solve the algorithms. Happy browsing!
